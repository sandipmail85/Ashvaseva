Services

Emergency Care – 24/7

1. Colic & digestive emergencies (pain relief, fluids, referral)

2. Wound & trauma care (suturing, bandaging, bleeding control)

3. Fracture & injury stabilization (splints, casts)

4. Laminitis & hoof pain (emergency hoof care)

5. Foaling emergencies (dystocia, assisted delivery)

6. Shock & collapse stabilization

Preventive & Routine Health

1. Annual vaccinations (Tetanus, Influenza, EHV, Rabies & more)

2. Deworming with fecal egg counts

3. Dental care – floating, wolf tooth removal

4. Health check-ups & blood tests

5. Coggins testing & microchipping for travel/competition

Performance Horse Care

1. Joint injections & therapies (corticosteroids, HA, PRP, IRAP)

2. Lameness evaluations (gait analysis, flexion tests, nerve blocks)

3. Regenerative medicine (stem cell, shockwave)

4. Hoof abscess drainage & farriery support

5. Performance monitoring (bloodwork, airway checks)

Diagnostic Services

1. Portable X-rays (fractures, bone, joint issues)

2. Ultrasound (tendon, ligament, reproduction)

3. Blood tests (CBC, infection, metabolic health)

4. Endoscopy (airway, gastric evaluation)

Reproduction & Foal Care

1. Emergency foaling & post-foaling care

2. Pregnancy diagnosis (portable ultrasound)

3. Retained placenta management

4. Foal resuscitation & neonatal support

Minor Surgical Procedures

1. Castration (gelding)

2. Tumor / sarcoid removal

3. Wound debridement for infection prevention

Additional Services

1. Insurance documentation & health certificates

2. Pre-purchase exams

3. Owner education (first aid, colic signs, wound care)

4. Teleconsultations & follow-up care
